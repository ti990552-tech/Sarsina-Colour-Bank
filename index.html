ABOUT US

Our Legacy of Quality

Sarsina Colour Bank has been a trusted name in the car paint industry since 2005. It has been a cornerstone of the automotive paint industry for over two decades. With years of experience, we have built a strong reputation based on honesty, quality, and customer satisfaction. Our journey began with a simple mission: to deliver the best products at reasonable prices while maintaining long-term relationships with our clients.
We proudly supply high-quality automotive paints, including MIDOMIX, one of the most trusted paint solutions in Bangladesh.

Our products are known for their excellent finish, strong durability, and reliable performance. At Sarsina Colour Bank, we always focus on maintaining product quality so our customers get the best results every time.
Today, we continue to serve our customers with dedication, offering reliable products at reasonable prices. Our mission is to ensure every customer receives the best value and performance from our products.
